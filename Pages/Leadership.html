Leadership and Activities

Marching Band Guide

Iowa State Marching Band

My time at Iowa State has been filled with many oppportunities for personal development and achievement. I was involved in the Iowa State Marching Band for 3 years, enjoying many home and away games with the Cyclones, and taking the position of guide in my third year. As part of this position, I worked with one other member of the band to lead a rank of 9 people, instructing them on proper marching technique and helping communicate the directions of band leadership.

My time in marching band also extended into basketball season, where I played with the mens basketball pep band for 3 years. This fantastic group was smaller and had a focus on higher quality sound unlike the visual focus found in marching band. This group was a highlight of my time in college, allowing me the oppportunity to travel to two Big-12 Basketball tournaments, the NCAA tournament, and watch the Iowa State Cyclones win the Big-12 Championship.

Rubik's Cube Competition Organizer

Since high school I have been actively involved in Rubik's Cube competitions. These events revolve around "cubers" coming together to solve Rubik's cubes of different shapes and sizes as quickly as they can. This great hobby has afforded me some wonderful oppportunities, such as making friends with people across the country and world, and traveling to multiple national tournaments to compete with the best of the best. Additionally, I have achieved many personal goals, such as solving a Rubik's cube in 7 seconds, obtaining multiple Iowa records, and ranking in the top 400 of speedcubers worldwide.

Beyond the competing, in recent years I have hosted my own competitions, to allow cubers from around the state and midwest to come enjoy this fantastic hobby. These competitions range from about 80 to 150 competitors, with total attendance exceeding 300 people. For my part as organizer, I was responsible for finding a venue, scheduling events, organizing a volunteer staff, and maintaining a budget to ensure a well run event.
